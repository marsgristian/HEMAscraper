[ 9v.2 ] Of the point.. Chapter VII.

[2] (P19.S1)
I am she who makes a caution to all strikes and call myself the point: I carry the poison like the scorpion. And I feel myself so strong, bold, and ready. Often I make the guards vary when others throw themselves at me and confront me. And by ill touch none when I have arrived.
